Privacy Policy

Ruthless Citation Generator — Chrome extension

Effective date: September 29, 2025

Overview

The Ruthless Citation Generator is a Chrome extension that generates Bluebook-formatted legal citations while you browse supported case law websites. We respect your privacy and keep things simple: the extension does not collect personal information.

Information Collection

  • The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
  • It reads case details visible on supported pages (e.g., case name, reporter, court, year) solely to create a citation.

Local Storage

The extension may save preferences (for example, a custom short case name) using Chrome’s local storage so your settings persist between sessions. This data stays on your device and is never shared.

Permissions Used

  • activeTab — access the current tab when you click the extension icon.
  • scripting — inject lightweight scripts on supported sites to read case data.
  • storage — store your preferences locally.
  • Host permissions — limited to specific legal research domains (e.g., Lexis, Westlaw) to read case metadata.

These permissions are used only to fulfill the extension’s single purpose.

Data Sharing

No personal data is collected and nothing is shared with third parties.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
